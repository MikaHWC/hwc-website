The short version

I'm a social media optimist with over a decade immersed in performance marketing.

My mission is simple: help businesses stop wasting money and guide them to take control of their paid social. I believe the industry works best when it's ethical and sustainable for everyone involved, business owners, agencies, and customers alike.

Through How We Convert, I champion a culture of "radical honesty" and empowerment. I'm passionate about leveraging AI and creative strategies not just for profit, but to create positive impacts and genuine human connections.

What I believe

Four principles that shape how I work.

01
Radical honesty
If your account doesn't need my help, I'll tell you. If a channel isn't working, I'll say so. No fluff, no vanity metrics, no pretending.
02
Own your system
The goal isn't for you to rely on me forever. It's to build a paid social operation you can run yourself, with AI as your copilot.
03
Ethical by default
Paid social works best when it genuinely serves the buyer. I don't work with founders who want to squeeze performance out of something people don't actually need.
04
AI + human judgement
AI handles the grunt work so the human (me, you) can focus on strategy, taste, and the judgement calls that actually move the needle.
