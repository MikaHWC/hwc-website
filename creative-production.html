The Creative Flywheel

The system that turns volume into winners.

01
Spy
Source proven concepts from brands a step above yours. A $1M brand studies the $10M players and the ads they have run the longest.
02
Produce
Recreate and adapt those concepts as video, statics, and carousels with the stack. Batch four to eight fresh creatives per ad set.
03
Force spend
Launch into a prospecting CBO and set a daily minimum equal to one times your target CPA. Let it run for exactly seven days.
04
Analyse
Sort by spend, compare incremental attribution, and find the concepts that command budget above your ROAS threshold.
05
Diversify
Duplicate, vary, and merge the winners. Run every profitable concept, because each one matches a different audience.
Then it loops. Every winner becomes the seed for the next batch, and the machine keeps growing as your spend grows.
