Questions

Common questions.

How is the commission paid?
Via Stripe, within 7 days of your referral's first payment. You receive 15% of their first month's subscription — that's $142.50 per Accelerate Program referral, or $127.50 per Scale Ongoing referral.
Is the commission recurring?
No, it's a one-time payment on the first month. Clean, simple, no ongoing reconciliation needed.
How do you track referrals?
You get a unique referral link when you're approved. Anyone who books an intro call through your link and signs up is attributed to you. We also accept manual introductions by email if you prefer.
What kind of businesses should I refer?
Ecommerce brands or lead generation businesses spending (or ready to spend) $5k to $10k a month on paid social. They should be willing to take the work in-house, not looking for someone to manage their account for them.
Do I need to be an agency to partner?
No. Anyone who sends qualified leads can partner with us. Agencies are the most common fit, but consultants, freelancers, and past HWC members are all welcome.
Can my referrals mention my name?
Absolutely. Manual attribution works perfectly. If they mention you on the intro call or in the form, you get credit regardless of which link they used.
