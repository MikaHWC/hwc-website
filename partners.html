Questions

Common questions.

How is the commission paid?
Via Stripe, within 7 days of your referral's first payment. You earn 15% of their first month: $142.50 for the Accelerate Program, $67.50 for Ongoing Scale, or $127.50 for AI Creative.
Is the commission recurring?
No. It is a single payment on the first month. Clean and simple, with no ongoing reconciliation needed.
Will How We Convert compete with my agency?
No. We coach brands to run paid social in-house. We do not offer a managed ad service, so we are not pitching the work you sell to the clients you keep.
How do you track referrals?
You get a unique referral link when you are approved. Anyone who books an intro call through your link and signs up is attributed to you. If they mention your name on the call, you still get credit.
What kind of businesses should I refer?
Ecommerce brands or lead generation businesses spending, or ready to spend, $5k to $10k a month on paid social. They should be open to taking the work in-house, not looking for someone to manage their account for them.
Do I need to be an agency to partner?
No. Anyone who sends qualified leads can partner with us. Full-service agencies are the most common fit, but past HWC members and other trusted referrers are welcome too.
