Creative is the new targeting

You can't feed the algorithm with two polished videos any more.

Meta and TikTok have taken targeting off your hands. Your job now is to feed their algorithms a constant stream of creative so they can find your buyers for you. Diversity and volume are the new levers.

Which is why paying $4K for a single old school video shoot no longer makes sense. You need several fresh concepts every month, testing angles, hooks and formats for every persona. Only AI production is fast and cheap enough to keep up.

The HWC creative team builds AI creatives for ecommerce and lead gen campaigns with a strategic mindset, personas, emotional purchase drivers, hook testing. Built to perform, not just to fill the feed.
