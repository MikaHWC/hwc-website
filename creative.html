Creative is the new targeting

You can't feed the algorithm with two polished videos any more.

Meta and TikTok have taken targeting off your hands. Your job now is to feed them a constant stream of fresh creative so the algorithm can find your buyers. Volume and diversity are the new levers.

That is what the Creative Flywheel does. A steady cycle of concepts, images, video and UGC, each one built to perform and tested against what is working. Not creative to fill the feed, creative to move the numbers.

Questions

Common questions.

Is this a standalone service?
No. Performance Creative Production is an add on to the Accelerate Program or Ongoing Scale. It runs alongside the coaching, feeding the system you are learning to run.
How custom is it?
The direction is fully custom every cycle, built around your personas, offers and what your data says is working. The deliverable volume is fixed, so you always know exactly what you are getting.
How many revisions do I get?
You approve the concept direction before any asset is produced, then get one revision on the finished work. Agreeing the direction upfront is what keeps the quality high without endless rounds.
Who finds the UGC creators?
I do. I source, vet and manage one long term micro creator partnership for you. Creator product and gifting are not included, you provide the product.
Will it look like AI?
No. Everything is reviewed before delivery, and anything that reads as AI generated does not ship. Built to perform, not to fill the feed with slop.
What is the commitment?
A three month cycle to protect the setup work, then month to month, cancel anytime.
